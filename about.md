MB Beekeeping is a practical beekeeping resource focused on cold-climate hive management. Based in Manitoba, Canada, we cover everything from getting your first hive through surviving harsh prairie winters with healthy colonies.

## What We Cover

Our guides are built around real experience keeping bees in northern conditions where temperatures regularly drop below -30C. We focus on actionable advice - specific temperatures, timelines, products, and techniques rather than vague generalities.

Topics include hive setup and equipment selection, winterization strategies for cold climates, seasonal management calendars, beginner-friendly how-to guides, and honest equipment reviews.

## Why MB Beekeeping

Most beekeeping content online is written for temperate climates. Northern beekeepers face a different set of challenges - shorter seasons, extreme cold, extended confinement periods, and moisture management issues that southern beekeepers never encounter. We write specifically for beekeepers dealing with these conditions.
